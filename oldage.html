VISION AND HEARING IMPAIRMENTS

Ayurveda offers several remedies that are believed to support vision and hearing impairments. However, it's important to note that these remedies are not substitutes for professional medical advice. If you're experiencing significant issues with your vision or hearing, it's crucial to consult with a qualified healthcare practitioner.

Solutions-

  • Triphala: Triphala is an Ayurvedic herbal preparation made from three fruits: Amalaki (Emblica officinalis), Bibhitaki (Terminalia bellirica), and Haritaki (Terminalia chebula). It is known for its antioxidant properties and may support eye health. You can take Triphala powder (1 teaspoon) with warm water at night before bed.
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter): Consuming a teaspoon of ghee daily is believed to be beneficial for eyesight. It can also be used externally as an anjana (eye treatment) by applying a small amount around the eyes.
  • Brahmi (Bacopa monnieri): Brahmi is an herb known for its rejuvenating properties. It's believed to support cognitive functions and may help in some cases of hearing impairment. Brahmi powder can be taken with honey or warm water.
  • Almond Milk: Soaking almonds overnight, removing the skin, and blending them with milk can be beneficial for vision health. You can drink this almond milk daily.
  • Anu Taila (Herbal Nasal Drops): Anu Taila is an Ayurvedic herbal oil used for nasal application. Some practitioners recommend using a few drops in each nostril to support sensory functions, including hearing.
  • Netra Tarpana (Eye Nourishment): This Ayurvedic therapy involves creating a dam-like structure around the eyes using black gram dough and then pouring medicated ghee into this structure. It's performed by Ayurvedic practitioners and is believed to nourish and strengthen the eyes.
